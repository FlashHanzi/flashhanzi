Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

FlashHanzi ("we", "our", or "us") operates the FlashHanzi mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

Information We Do Not Collect

FlashHanzi does not collect any personally identifiable information. The App does not require account creation or submission of personal data.

We do not collect or store any personal data, including but not limited to your name, email address, contacts, or location.

Information We Collect

To provide features like camera and handwriting recognition, FlashHanzi may request access to device capabilities such as:

  • Camera access (to scan Chinese characters)
  • Storage access (to save user data locally)

This data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted or shared with third parties.

Subscriptions and Purchases

If you choose to subscribe to premium features through in-app purchases, your purchase information is handled securely by the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. We do not collect or store payment information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy here.
